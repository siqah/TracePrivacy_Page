1. Introduction

Welcome to Trace ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mental wellness and mood tracking application.

We understand that mental health data is sensitive. We have built Trace to ensure that you remain the sole owner and custodian of your data.

2. Information Storage

2.1 Information Is Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers: We actually do not have any servers.

  • Mood Data: Ratings, emotions, and timestamps.
  • Journal Entries: Personal notes and reflections.
  • Wellness Activities: Records of breathing exercises and meditation.
  • Preferences: Notification settings, themes, and reminders.
  • Authentication: PINs or biometric tokens (stored in the device's Secure Enclave/Keystore).
  • Analytics: Trends and statistics calculated locally on your phone.

2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

To ensure your privacy, we expressly do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • GPS or precise location data.
  • Contact lists or photo libraries.
  • Usage data via third-party tracking scripts.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since data remains on your device, the App processes it locally to:

  • Visualize your mood history and identifying patterns.
  • Generate personalized wellness insights.
  • Schedule local notifications and reminders.
  • Generate exportable PDF/CSV reports (initiated only by you).

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Encryption

Your data is stored in a local SQLite database protected by your device's operating system sandbox. We utilize standard OS-level security features to prevent unauthorized access by other applications.

4.2 No Cloud Sync

Trace does not automatically sync to the cloud. There is no central server containing user data, eliminating the risk of a remote mass data breach.

4.3 Backups

Your data may be included in your personal device backups (e.g., iCloud, Google Drive). This is managed by your operating system settings and is outside the control of the Trace application.

5. Permissions

We request minimal permissions to function:

  • Notifications: To send reminders (optional).
  • Biometrics (FaceID/TouchID): To lock the app for additional privacy (optional).

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data. Your data never leaves your device unless you manually use the "Export" feature to share a report with a healthcare provider or save it to your files.

7. Children's Privacy

Trace is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect info from children. If you believe a child has used the app, simply uninstalling the app from their device will delete all associated data.

8. User Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all history within the app.
  • Export: Generate reports of your data.
  • Deletion: Delete specific entries or clear all data via App Settings.
  • Revocation: Uninstalling the app permanently removes the local database.
